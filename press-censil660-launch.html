Censilcoating Launches Censil660 High-Clarity Silica Matting Agent for Southeast Asian Waterborne Wood Coatings

New grade delivers water-white transparency at matte gloss levels, positioned as a 1:1 drop-in replacement for Evonik ACEMATT OK 412 and Grace SYLOID 244.

15 July 2026 | 4 min read

For Immediate Release

HO CHI MINH CITY, Vietnam — 15 July 2026 — Censilcoating, the waterborne silica matting agent brand of Censil, today announced the launch of Censil660, a high-clarity precipitated silica matting agent engineered specifically for waterborne wood and plastic coatings. The new grade is now available with free 2 kg evaluation samples for coating manufacturers in Vietnam and Malaysia.

Censil660 features a narrow particle-size distribution centred at 5.0–5.3 µm (d50), with a pore volume of 1.8 mL/g and an organic surface treatment optimized for waterborne acrylic and polyurethane dispersions. In side-by-side benchmark testing, Censil660 matched Evonik ACEMATT OK 412 and Grace SYLOID 244 across all critical parameters: 60° gloss at equal dosage, water-white film clarity on dark substrates, viscosity stability over 30 days, and scratch resistance.

Addressing a gap in the Southeast Asian supply chain

Waterborne wood coating production in Vietnam and Malaysia has grown rapidly, driven by furniture export demand and environmental regulations phasing out solventborne systems. However, formulators in the region have historically relied on imported European matting agents, facing lead times of 6–8 weeks, minimum order quantities designed for large-scale buyers, and pricing that erodes competitiveness.

"Censil660 was built to close that gap," said Jackson Jiang, Export Manager at Censilcoating. "We spent eighteen months in benchmark development to ensure that a formulator can switch from OK 412 or SYLOID 244 to Censil660 without adjusting their formulation, their process, or their quality control limits. Same dosage, same gloss, same clarity — shorter lead time and a better price."

Free evaluation program

To support formulators in validating Censil660 in their own systems, Censilcoating offers a free 2 kg evaluation sample shipped with:

  • A 1:1 physicochemical benchmark sheet (English and Chinese)
  • The relevant Technical Data Sheet (TDS)
  • A REACH Declaration of Conformity
  • Recommended starting dosage and dispersion protocol

The typical evaluation cycle is 1–2 weeks from sample receipt to final drawdown confirmation. Censilcoating's technical team provides remote formulation support throughout the trial.

About Censilcoating

Censilcoating is the waterborne-matting sub-brand of Censil, a precipitated silica manufacturer headquartered in Fujian, China, with an annual capacity of 40,000 tonnes. The Censilcoating range comprises seven coating-grade silica matting agents, each mapped 1:1 to a specific Evonik ACEMATT or Grace SYLOID benchmark grade. The brand serves waterborne wood and plastic coating formulators in Vietnam, Malaysia, and across Southeast Asia.
